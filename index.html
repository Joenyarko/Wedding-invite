Our Story

In the symphony of life, there are rare moments when time seems to pause—when everything aligns so perfectly that it feels as though heaven itself had a hand in the making. For me, Everett, one such moment came unexpectedly during one of the most spiritually uplifting events of my life—a special Witnesses Convention of Jehovah’s Witnesses at the Nyanyanu assembly grounds. I had only planned to attend in support of my auntie.

With my camera in hand, my original mission was simple: to capture joyful memories of my family. But in a twist that can only be described as divine, the lens of my camera was drawn to someone far more radiant—Mavis. I remember it so clearly: the soft glow of the afternoon light, the warmth in the air, and then her—a picture of grace, poise, and a smile that seemed to reach straight into me.

It wasn’t just a photograph. When I went back to view the pictures from that day, Mavis’s smile stood out—vivid, vibrant, almost alive. It was more than an image; it was a moment frozen in time that kept replaying in my heart. That smile—pure, effortless, unforgettable—was the beginning of something I couldn’t quite explain, but deeply felt.

I didn’t know then that fate wasn’t done with us. As though orchestrated by a higher purpose, our paths crossed again—not in a crowded auditorium, but in a humble setting during a voluntary evangelism assignment in Wa. And that’s where everything truly began.

Meeting May May again in such a selfless, spiritual environment only deepened my admiration for her. I got to see the woman behind the smile—strong, gentle, committed, and full of light. She wasn’t just beautiful in the fleeting way the world sees beauty; hers was a beauty that flowed from within, wrapped in humility and grace.

From our conversations under the sun to shared laughter amidst the dust of our mission, I discovered a connection I had never felt before. Mavis wasn’t just a kindred spirit—she was home. Every step we took from that point was a step closer to a love I never expected, yet had longed for all my life.

Our journey hasn’t been without its trials, but each challenge only solidified our bond. Through uncertainty and distance, through silent prayers and shared dreams, we found in each other something rare and lasting—a love nurtured by faith and purpose.

And so, the hashtag **#Mayeverafter** is more than a memory; it’s a symbol of where it all began. It marks the turning point in our lives when a simple photograph turned into a lifelong story, and when a smile turned into a promise of forever.

Today, as we look ahead to the future hand-in-hand, I do so with a heart overflowing with gratitude. For in Mavis, I have found more than a partner—I’ve found my answered prayer, my lifelong companion, and the most beautiful blessing I never saw coming.

Our story is one of quiet miracles, divine timing, and the extraordinary found in the everyday. And as we prepare to walk the path of forever, I carry this truth with me: that love, when rooted in faith and nurtured by sincerity, truly does conquer all.
